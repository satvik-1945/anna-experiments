    Local data

    ResuMatch keeps everything on your computer. Each Fetch jobs run replaces the previous job cache and rebuilds tailored resumes for the new results. Your application statuses (Saved, Applied, etc.) stay in the browser until you clear site data.

    Job cache
    ~/.anna/resumatch/jobs_cache.json
    Latest fetch
    ~/.anna/resumatch/jobs_latest.json
    Your listings
    ~/.anna/resumatch/application_packs.json
    Tailored resumes
    ~/.anna/resumatch/resumes/
    Compiled PDFs
    ~/.anna/resumatch/pdfs/ and ~/Downloads/
    Profile
    ~/.anna/resumatch/profile.json

    To free disk space after you've applied, quit ResuMatch and delete ~/.anna/resumatch/ (or just resumes/, pdfs/, and application_packs.json). Then fetch again. Your profile is preserved unless you delete profile.json.
